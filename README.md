# ☕ BunnBeen Coffee Sales Analysis: Unlocking India's Next Coffee Hotspots
**Strategic SQL Analysis of BunnBeen Coffee's Online Sales (Jan 2023 - Present)**  
*Data-driven recommendations for 3 prime Indian cities to launch physical coffee shops*

## 🎯 Business Objective
BunnBeen Coffee has dominated **online sales** since January 2023. Now they need to conquer **physical locations**.  

**My Mission**: Analyze 2+ years of sales data using advanced PostgreSQL queries to identify the **Top 3 Indian cities** for new coffee shop openings based on:
- Consumer demand patterns
- Revenue performance
- Product popularity
- Seasonal trends
- Growth potential

## Approach
- 10 Strategic Business Questions answered with production-grade SQL
- Real-world dataset (online orders, customers, products, locations)

## Questions

1. Coffee Consumers Count given that 25% of the population does.

2. Total Revenue from Coffee Sales across all cities in the last quarter of 2023.
  
3. Sales Count for Each Product

4. Average Sales Amount per City per customer

5. City Population and Coffee Consumers per city

6. Top Selling Products by City based on sales volume.

7. Customer Segmentation by City

8. Average Sale vs Rent per customer

9. Monthly Sales Growth

10. Market Potential Analysis ( top 3 city based on highest sales, return city name, total sale, total rent, total customers, estimated coffee consumer)
